# CloudPath Academy

**Your guide to AWS certification success**

---

Studying for AWS certifications is straightforward — work through the exam guide, watch some videos, read a book, take practice tests. But passing an exam and truly understanding the technology are two very different things. When I began preparing, I kept running into the same frustration: study materials told me *what* AWS services do, but rarely explained *why* they were designed that way, or when to choose one over another. That gap sent me digging directly into the source — AWS documentation, service FAQs, and the architectural whitepapers that AWS engineers use to explain their own design decisions. The problem was that tracking down the right documents often took longer than the actual studying. I'd spend more time hunting for a whitepaper than reading it. This site is the organized result of that digging — so you don't have to repeat it.

Every study guide on CloudPath Academy is built around the official AWS exam domain structure, but the real value is in the curated links to primary sources. Each domain page points directly to the AWS documentation, FAQ entries, and whitepapers most relevant to that exam objective — the same resources I used to move from "I know the answer" to "I understand why this is the right answer." I've also included AWS Skill Builder links for hands-on labs and official training. These guides save me countless hours every time I start a new certification, and I hope they do the same for you.
